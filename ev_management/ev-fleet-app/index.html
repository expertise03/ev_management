Platform Features

Everything You Need to Run a Smarter Fleet

From real-time telemetry to cost analytics, VoltFleet gives you complete control.

🔋

Battery Health Monitor

Track battery percentage, temperature, voltage, and charging cycles. Get alerts before battery health degrades.

📍

Real-Time Location

Live GPS tracking of all vehicles. See speed, route, and remaining range on an interactive map view.

🧠

Driver Behaviour Analytics

Score drivers on harsh braking, sudden acceleration, and overspeeding to improve safety and efficiency.

⚙️

Predictive Maintenance

Maintenance alerts based on service history, tire health, and component wear to prevent costly breakdowns.

💡

Energy Cost Analysis

Daily, monthly, and per-trip energy cost breakdowns. Optimize charging schedules to reduce expenses.

🗺️

Route Optimisation

Smart route suggestions with traffic-aware ETA, charging stops, and road type classification.

📊

Custom Reports

Generate fleet-wide or per-vehicle reports on energy consumption, driver scores, and maintenance costs.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Separate dashboards for fleet managers and drivers with fine-grained permissions and email verification.

How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes

A simple three-step process to get your fleet online.

1

Register Your Account

Admins register with their Manager ID and key. Drivers sign up with their fleet manager's credentials.

2

Add Your Vehicles

Monitor in Real-Time

About VoltFleet

Built for the Future of Mobility

VoltFleet is a purpose-built fleet management platform for electric vehicles. We help fleet managers reduce downtime, cut energy costs, and keep drivers safe.

  • Supports all major EV brands — Tata, MG, Ather, Ola Electric, and more
  • Real-time alerts when battery health drops below threshold
  • Comprehensive driver scoring and coaching insights
  • Seamless integration with charging station networks across India
  • GDPR-compliant data handling with encrypted credentials
